# Roommate Harmony - Pitch Deck & Demo Script

## Slide 1 — Cover
- **Visual:** Project Name: Roommate Harmony. Tagline: "Shared living clarity powered by AI". Clean minimal layout.
- **Script:** "Hi everyone, we are team [Name] and this is Roommate Harmony — a social awareness assistant for shared living."

## Slide 2 — The Problem
- **Visual:** "Shared living is quietly breaking down."
- **Script:** "We've all had roommates. We know the friction. Chores pile up unevenly, expenses get messy, and participation fades. The crazy thing is, nobody notices until it becomes a real fight. There are no tools for household social awareness."

## Slide 3 — The Insight
- **Visual:** "The problem isn't bad roommates. It's missing data."
- **Script:** "Our core insight is this: tension doesn't come from bad intentions. It comes from invisible patterns. When everyone has a different idea of what's fair, arguments happen."

## Slide 4 — Our Solution
- **Visual:** "Roommate Harmony — a social awareness assistant" + UI Dashboard screenshot.
- **Script:** "Roommate Harmony provides that shared picture. It tracks chores, expenses, and participation, and uses an AI layer to catch imbalances early."

## Slide 5 — How It Works
- **Visual:** 5 Steps: Log -> Analyze -> Score -> Alert -> Mediate.
- **Script:** "It's simple. Roommates log their activity. The AI analyzes the patterns and generates a household stability score. If someone's participation drops, it triggers a soft alert and offers mediation suggestions."

## Slide 6 — Key Features
- **Visual:** 4 Pillars (Mediation, Expenses, Analytics, Alerts).
- **Script:** "We built four tools: an AI Mediation assistant to remove emotional charge, a shared expense dashboard, participation analytics, and early warning alerts to catch problems before they explode."

## Slide 7 — Live Demo
- **Visual:** (Switch to the React App — scroll through the Dashboard Snapshot)
- **Script:** "Let's look at the Maple Street House. You can see their stability score is 74 (Moderate). But wait, we have an alert. Alex's participation dropped 34%. He's only done 2 of 8 chores. Because we catch this early, the AI suggests a neutral chore rotation, instead of letting resentments build up."

## Slide 8 — Target Users
- **Visual:** "Anyone sharing a space"
- **Script:** "This isn't just for college kids. It's for young professionals, co-living communities, and long-term housemates whose dynamics have shifted."

## Slide 9 — Why This Matters
- **Visual:** "Social awareness is the missing layer"
- **Script:** "Venmo handles the money. Roommate Harmony handles the relationships. It's the first pure social awareness layer for shared spaces."

## Slide 10 — Constraints & Scope
- **Visual:** "Built with clear boundaries"
- **Script:** "We specifically avoided complex financial or legal integrations. This is purely about social awareness and AI insights."

## Slide 11 & 12 — Tech Stack & Team
- **Visual:** React, Tailwind, Node, AI Pattern Engine.
- **Script:** "We built this using React and Tailwind on the frontend, with an AI pattern recognition engine to calculate stability scores and generate mediation prompts."

## Slide 13 — Closing
- **Visual:** "Shared living should feel fair. Now it can."
- **Script:** "Shared living doesn't have to require a mediator, but sometimes it needs one. Thank you."
